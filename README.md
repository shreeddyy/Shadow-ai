Shadow AI – Intelligent Decision Assistant

Shadow AI is an AI-powered decision-making assistant that helps users analyze their choices by combining emotional context with rational AI analysis.
It uses Google Gemini API to provide calm, structured, and thoughtful advice for real-life decisions.

“When emotions cloud judgment, Shadow AI brings clarity.”

 Problem Statement

People often make important decisions when they are:

Emotionally overwhelmed

Confused or anxious

Under time pressure

Traditional AI tools give generic answers and ignore emotional context, which can lead to poor or impulsive decisions.
